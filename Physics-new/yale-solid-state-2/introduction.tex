\section{Band theory}

To arrive at the band theory, we assume:
\begin{itemize}
    \item No relativistic effects
    (except when deriving pseudopotentials or calculating SOC);
    \item Born-Oppenheimer approximation: 
    putting electron-phonon interaction aside
    \item Electrons don't talk to each other:
    leaving the Coulomb correction aside 
\end{itemize}

Fermions in solid state physics include electrons
and what derive from them, including 
holes and polarons.
