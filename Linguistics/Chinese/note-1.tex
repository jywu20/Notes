Mandarin is generally regarded as a prototypical analytic language,
without traditionally acknowledged verb inflections.
Indeed it will be wired to posit something like a paradigm in Mandarin,
but it doesn't mean there is no such thing as verbal suffixes that are active
in the morphosyntax 
(instead of largely historical derivations).
